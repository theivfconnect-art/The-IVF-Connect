Proprietary Framework

The Clinical
Translation Method™

1

Understand

We begin by reviewing your complete fertility history — every result, every report, every cycle — to build a clear picture of where you are and what has been missed or misunderstood.

2

Interpret

I translate the clinical language — AMH, FSH, morphology, blastocyst grading, protocol terminology — into plain-language insights that give your results real meaning for your specific situation.

3

Strategise

Using the S.T.A.R.T Framework™, we build a personalised strategy — what to optimise, what to question, what to prepare for — before a single R1 of treatment is spent.

4

Prepare

We prepare you to walk into your clinic fully equipped — with informed questions, a clear understanding of your protocol, and the confidence to advocate for yourself at every appointment.

5

Navigate

I stay with you through your cycle — translating fertilisation reports, embryo updates, and lab communications in real time — so you are never left guessing at the most important moments.
